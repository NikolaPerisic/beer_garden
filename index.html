About

Beer Garden takes great pride in giving our guests a great selection of food & beverages. Along with our full service bar featuring Local Brew, Imported, Domestic, & Premium Beers. We also carry more than 50 different Craft beers. Now is a great time to stop by and enjoy some of the fantastic craft beer that is available. Our own Pacific craft brew is specially designed and made on the island, using the top quality imported hops from USA, Europe and Asia. With great selection of foods and beverages, unique relaxing atmosphere and our friendly staff, Beer Garden is a must stop for every island visitor.

  • High Standard
  • All kinds of brews
